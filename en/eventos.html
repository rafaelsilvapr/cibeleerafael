Frequently asked questions

Before you message on WhatsApp.

How much does it cost to hire the duo?

Each proposal is tailor-made — event type, duration, city and venue structure all change the equation. That's why we do not work with a public rate card: you tell us about your event and receive a proposal designed for it.

Do you bring sound equipment?

The base format is acoustic and intimate — perfect for social spaces and places with good acoustics. When the event needs larger sound reinforcement, the structure enters the proposal in partnership with regional suppliers.

How long does the performance last?

The most common format is up to 3 hours of music, distributed according to the event schedule (reception, dinner, toast...). Different durations are agreed in the proposal.

Can the repertoire be personalized?

Yes — that is the house specialty. The setlist is born from the occasion, and special requests (the couple's song, the family's song, the company's atmosphere) are part of the process.

Do you perform outside Bagé?

Yes. We work in Bagé and the surrounding region — Pelotas, Dom Pedrito, Sant'Ana do Livramento, Candiota, Aceguá, Hulha Negra, Lavras do Sul and nearby areas. Other cities on request, with travel included in the proposal.

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings and busy dates, a few months in advance helps secure the date. For smaller events, it is worth asking — whenever possible, we fit it in.
