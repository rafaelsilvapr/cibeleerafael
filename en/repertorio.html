Formats

From intimate to danceable.

House format

Acoustic duo — voice & guitar

The duo's base format: elegant musical presence, at a conversation-friendly volume, tailor-made for receptions, dinners, weddings and corporate events. It does not require heavy venue structure.

On request

Expanded lineup

For events that ask for a dance floor, the duo builds larger lineups with guest musicians and a sound structure suited to the space. Tell us what you imagine — the proposal comes designed for the occasion.

Musical program

The Brazil you hear — special shows

For special occasions, the duo creates musical scripts that tell a story through the songbook — an era, a theme, a journey across the country. A reception performance designed for the event's atmosphere.
