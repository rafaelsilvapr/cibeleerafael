Cibele e Rafael

a musical journey across Brazil

Cibele e Rafael is a Brazilian live music duo — voice and guitar — specializing in weddings, receptions and corporate events. Based in Bagé/RS, Brazil; available for performances across Brazil and internationally. Musical direction: Rafael da Silva, PhD in Music (UFRGS) and professor at Unipampa.

The proposal

Songs that bring people together from north to south, with rhythm you feel in your body and the sophistication your event calls for.

Many Brazils to create the right atmosphere.

From samba to xote, from bossa to baião — a Brazilian repertoire to bring people closer, guide the room's energy and strengthen the mood the occasion needs to create.

Weddings · Receptions · Corporate events

The duo

Voice, guitar and a real feel for the room.

Cibele Martinez brings the warm presence of the voice. Rafael da Silva drives the guitar with rhythmic pulse, in an acoustic and intimate format that lets the swing show without losing elegance.

Together, they understand the event in real time: they welcome, energize, bring people closer and adjust the music's energy to what the occasion asks for.
